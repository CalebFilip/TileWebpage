About Us


With over 20 years of experience in the industry, J&J Marble, Granite, and Ceramics is a trusted name for high-quality residential and custom tile projects. Solely owned and operated by John Filip, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship in every project, from intricate backsplash designs to durable granite and ceramics installations. Serving the beautiful North Carolina mountains and piedmont regions, we take pride in creating spaces that combine elegance and functionality. At J&J, customer satisfaction and attention to detail are at the heart of everything we do.

Services


At J&J Marble, Granite, and Ceramics, we offer a range of specialized services to transform your home with beautiful and durable stonework. With a focus on quality and craftsmanship, we provide:

  • Marble and Granite Installation:

    Expertly crafted and installed countertops, backsplashes, and flooring, using premium marble and granite to enhance the beauty and value of your home.

  • Custom Tile Work:

    Tailored designs for bathrooms, kitchens, and other spaces, incorporating unique patterns and high-quality materials to meet your specific needs.

  • Ceramic and Porcelain Tile Installation:

    From classic to contemporary styles, we offer precision installation of ceramic and porcelain tiles for walls, floors, and other surfaces.

  • Residential Projects:

    Specializing in home renovations and remodels, we bring attention to detail and a commitment to craftsmanship to every project.

  • Custom Designs:

    Whether you have a vision in mind or need guidance, we work with you to create personalized designs that perfectly fit your style and space.

Serving the North Carolina mountains and piedmont, J&J is your go-to partner for all marble, granite, and ceramics projects, ensuring high-quality results and customer satisfaction.
